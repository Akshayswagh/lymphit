Post-Surgical Swelling & Lymphatic Support

Healing Well Starts with the Right Support

Swelling is a natural part of the healing process after surgery, but when it becomes excessive or prolonged, it can slow recovery, cause discomfort, and impact results. Whether you've had orthopedic surgery, cosmetic procedures, cancer-related surgery, or other operations, your lymphatic system plays a key role in clearing swelling and waste from the body.

Why Does Post-Surgical Swelling Happen?

Surgery often involves cutting through tissues and blood vessels, which leads to inflammation and fluid buildup. When the lymphatic system struggles to keep up with this excess fluid, swelling can linger and even lead to complications like fibrosis (hardening of tissues) or limited range of motion.

The Role of Lymphatic Therapy in Recovery

Lymphatic support can help speed up healing, reduce swelling, minimize scarring, and improve overall comfort during recovery. Our clinic offers gentle, specialized care to support your body as it heals.

Our Post-Surgical Services Include:

Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD)

A light, rhythmic massage technique designed to stimulate lymph flow and reduce swelling.

Education & Self-Care Guidance

Learn how to protect your healing tissues and support your lymphatic health at home.

Customized Compression Solutions

Compression garments or wraps tailored to your surgical site to manage swelling and support healing.

Monitoring & Prevention

Ongoing support to help prevent chronic swelling or lymphedema, especially after procedures involving lymph node removal or radiation.

Common Surgeries We Support:

Mastectomy or lumpectomy
Liposuction and cosmetic procedures
Joint replacements
Orthopedic surgeries
Lymph node removal
Biopsies

A Gentle, Supportive Approach to Healing

Every surgical journey is unique. We work closely with your surgical team (or independently) to create a plan that supports your body's natural healing process and helps you feel your best—faster and more comfortably.
